Our Associate

Ex Sub. Ajit Patil, having a vivid corporate and defense experience, has taken a step ahead to associate with Quasar. He has the honor to serve in major defense operations like "OP Vijay Kargil" and "OP Pawan Sri Lanka" and has continued to serve the nation after hanging up his uniform. Post-retirement, he pursued BA, LLB, DIBM, and DPM, and has taken the onus of ex-service man welfare. He is holding the position of vice chairman of the National Ex-Servicemen Coordination Committee and working as an advisor of Veteran India Maharashtra unit. On the corporate front, he has vast experience in man management and has a fleet of school buses to serve various schools. Besides his outstanding work in social and educational fields, he has been awarded the "Vishesh Gaurav" Puraskar by the Govt of Maharashtra and "Sanman Chinh" by the Pune Municipal Corporation for outstanding social work. He has served for 26 years in military intelligence and is capable of handling security matters for societies as well as industries. Mr. Ajit Patil and Quasar have united their efforts and are channel partners in business development.
